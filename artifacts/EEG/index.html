Ocular eye blink

What you will see

Large, slow deflection with a frontal maximum; often clearly visible in vertical EOG channels.

Judgement calls and decision-making considerations

  • Did the blink occur during a critical period?
  • Is it isolated or frequent?
  • Is the frontal activity clearly separable from the neural signal?
  • Would rejecting all blink trials leave enough trials in every condition?

Possible solution and response

  • Improve participant instructions.
  • Mark and reject affected epochs.
  • Use ICA or regression when appropriate.
  • Retain unaffected portions of continuous data.
